Sorghum bicolor inflorescence and seeds.
  • Available gene ID format for this experiment: Sobic.***G****** or Sb**g******
  • Data from Davidson et al., 2012.
  • Sorghum samples were collected from 20 days-old leaves, inflorescences before flower emergence, inflorescence at flower emergence, pistils and anthers while anthesis, seeds 5 and 10 days after pollination and embryos and endosperms from seeds 25 days after pollination. Samples were subjected to RNAseq analysis.
  • The criteria for the identification of a tissue-enriched gene was a normalized expression in a particular tissue above the 175% of average expression value accross all tissues.
  • Genes with no expression were removed.
  • TOTEM browser uses a two-tailed Fisher's test in its enrichment function.
