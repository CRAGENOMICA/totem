Solanum lycopersicum fruit development map.
  • Available gene ID format for this experiment: Solyc**g******
  • Data from Shinozaki et al., 2018.
  • Tomato fruits spanning all ripening process from anthesis to comprete ripening were collected and hand-dissected or lasser-microdissected. The different tissues, at different develepment stages and different zones (stem, equatorial and stylar regions) were submited for RNAseq analysis.
  • The criteria for the identification of a sample-enriched gene was a normalized expression in a particular sample above the 175% of average normalized expression value accross all samples.
  • SVG image only represents equatorial regions of tomato fruit.
  • TOTEM browser uses a two-tailed Fisher's test in its enrichment function.
