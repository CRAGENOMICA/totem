Arabidopsis thaliana wild-type leaves with single cell RNAseq resolution.
  • Available gene ID format for this experiment: AT*G*****
  • Leaf scRNAseq atlas with 11413 cells constructed by integrating Kim et al., 2021 and Lopez-Anido et al., 2021 datasets.
  • Tissue identification with experimentally-tested marker genes and pseudotime analysis reveals 34 different cell populations in different developmental stages in the Arabidopsis leaves.
  • A gene is cell population-enriched if its expression is at least the double comparing the rest of the cell populations, if it is expressed in at least the 25% of all cells in the atlas, if its p-value is less than 0,01 and if its log2 fold change is higher than 0.58.
